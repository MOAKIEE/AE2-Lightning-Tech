The **Overload Alloy** is an advanced metallic material used in many high-tier recipes.

## Processing Chain

1. **Overload Alloy Blank** — the raw intermediate form
2. **Overload Alloy** — the finished alloy ingot, refined from the blank
3. **Overload Alloy Plate** — pressed from the Overload Alloy, used in several end-tier crafting recipes

## Items

| Item | Role |
|------|------|
| Overload Alloy Blank | Raw intermediate product |
| Overload Alloy | Finished alloy ingot, a key crafting material |
| Overload Alloy Plate | Pressed plate form, used in advanced recipes such as the Ultimate Overload Core |
